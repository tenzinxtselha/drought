IMPACTS

Droughts affect many aspects of the environment

Water levels tend to decrease putting the aquatic wild life in danger

Kills wildlife that depend on water. For example, fish levels. Without eater, they cannot survive

Plant life. Water is a necessity for plants. Without water, they dry up

SOLUTIONS

CONSERVE WATER

The best possible way to prevent drought is to save water. Take shorter showers, use less water, don't leave the tap running. There are many ways to conserve water. Try them all
