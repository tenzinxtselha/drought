WHAT IS DROUGHT?

The definition of drought is the prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water In other words, drought means a long period of no water

WHY IS IT SO IMPORTANT?

Water is a necessity in everyday life. It feeds the plants and animals. The main source of water is from rainfall. Without rainfall, plants, animals, and living organisms die out.
